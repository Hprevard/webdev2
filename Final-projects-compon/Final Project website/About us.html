About Us

Welcome to Celestial Connections where we strive to be enlightened and seek higher knowledge that brings us closer to the universe. Celestial Connections was started by our leader Terra Jones. Raised in a christian church she started to see her true purpose to lead and to study taboo and occult topics. After studying tarot cards, astrology, runes and other subjects for 5 years she left the church and started her own. In our home in the beautiful wooded area of Crossett, Arkansas we have a wonderful Lodge built by Terra and the other members just a few years ago and we all live wonderful lives here. Our days in our community include doing guided meditations, tarot readings, astrology observations and readings. We also have sound bowl meditations, reiki rituals and many more. Recording all of it so that we can share and educate all of our followers. Our mission is to guide those on their life path to achieve their higher purpose and to stay aligned with their higher self. Using the resources we provide you have the best available knowledge during your journey. We are well aware of the misinformation that reigns heavily in the spiritual community which is why we use information from peer reviewed sources and from spiritualists who have spent many years on the topics.
